Who is Hippo Campus?

Hippo Campus is an indie rock band that cranks out some insanely good music. They started out in 2013 after the members bumped into each other at a music school (but the name actually came from a part of the brain, which they got when one of the band members took a physiology class). Their earlier songs are very experimental with how they organize the music out, while anything they made past 2018 tends to be more refined yet fresh at the same time. These people just keep on kicking ass and creating some of the best songs in history.

And oh, their concerts are kinda legendary.
